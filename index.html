About

Welcome to the Computer Science Club! We are a student-driven organization dedicated to exploring technology, building skills, and making a positive impact on our school and community. Through five specialized teams—Game Design & Development, Competitions, 3D Printing, Website, and AWS—students have the opportunity to learn real-world skills in coding, design, cybersecurity, cloud computing, and prototyping. We provide hands-on experiences that combine creativity, problem-solving, and teamwork. Club members collaborate on projects that serve the community, support school groups, and tackle meaningful challenges, all while gaining practical knowledge that prepares them for future careers in technology. Whether it is developing games, creating adaptive devices, building and maintaining websites, competing in programming challenges, or managing cloud infrastructure, students can apply their learning in real-world contexts and make a tangible difference.
