About

The SJC CompSci Club aims to inspire students to utilize their interests in varying fields of computer science for service-based projects. Members of our club who are interested in web design work on creating websites for SJC clubs-- including ourselves. Students with interest in 3D modeling design and print medical adaptive devices. The SJC fish tank's interactive learning app is also credited to our app development team!
